Tangzhong Rolls

Fluffy, buttery, savory, n salty

Ingredients & Steps

8x8 pan (9 rolls)

tangzhong

Combine, heat while stirring until thickened

Stand mixer with dough hook : mix on low until shaggy dough forms

Mix on medium for 8-10 minutes, until smooth supple dough forms

Shape dough into ball and proof until roughly doubled (1–1.5 hours)

Grease pan with butter and place in fridge

( Laminate in chives )

Divide dough and shape

Cold proof in pan for up to 24 hours

Rise at room temp (2+ hours)

375° : 25–30 min / internal 190°

Sources

Claire Saffitz

Notes

  • If top is browned before inside is done, cover with tin foil
