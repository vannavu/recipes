Phở

rainy day choice

Ingredients & Steps

6–8 servings

meat

In a 12-qt pot, hardboil the meat for 2–3 minutes

Rinse and scrub the meat

Clean the pot

Return meat to the pot


stock vegetables

Broil until charred


spices

Toast on the stove until aromatic

Place all spices and vegetables into a cloth bag and add to the stock pot

Bring to a boil then reduce heat to a very low simmer

Leave to simmer uncovered, occasionally stirring and skimming the fat

Remove brisket once tender (~1.5 hours), cover in cold water, slice once cool, and refrigerate until ready to serve

Continue cooking broth until oxtail is tender (~6 hours)

Remove oxtail and cloth bag

Strain broth into another pot

Return meat into the pot


Season with any additional salt and rock sugar
