Dappled Date Cake

effortlessly deep, fragrant flavor that’s not too sweet

Ingredients & Steps

8" round / 8 cupcakes

Trim parchment paper for the bottom of the pan

Grease entire pan with coconut oil

Coat the sides of the pan with sesame seeds


Sprinkle sugar over bottom of the pan


Tear dates in half, remove pit, and arrange skin side down in the bottom of the pan

dry ingredients

Sift

Preheat oven to 350°

wet ingredients

Whisk

Whisk in dry ingredients

Pour batter into pan

Bake until center of cake looks set and springs back
• 8" round : 30 minutes
• cupcakes : 18 minutes

Cool for 5 minutes

soak & salt

Mix coconut milk and vanilla

Pour coconut milk soak over cake

Once soak is absorbed, invert cake onto serving dish


Finish with flaky salt

Sprinkle leftover sesame seeds from the pan onto the top of the cake

Sources

Natasha Pickowicz : More Than Cake : Dappled Date Cake

Notes

  • Gently reheat leftovers
  • Serve with tea or milk
