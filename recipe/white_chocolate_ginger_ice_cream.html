White Chocolate & Fresh Ginger Ice Cream

As slammin’ as it sounds

Ingredients & Steps

3 cups

Heat until simmering

Cover and steep for 1 hour

Gradually whisk milk mixture into yolks

Stir and heat mixture to 175°

Turn off heat and incorporate cornstarch slurry

Strain custard into chocolate and mix

Cool, chill, churn
