Sour Cream & Chive Rolls

Fluffy, buttery, savory, n salty

Ingredients & Steps

8x8 pan

tangzhong

Whisk and heat until thickened

Mix on low until shaggy dough forms

Mix on medium for 8-10 minutes, until smooth supple dough forms

Shape dough into ball and proof until roughly doubled (1–1.15 hours)

Grease pan with 1 tbsp of butter and place in fridge

Laminate in chives

Divide dough and shape

Cold proof for up to 24 hours

Bring to room temp and allow to rise (2+ hours)

375° : 25–30 min
