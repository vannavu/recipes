Black Bean Quinoa Balls

ideally for spaghetti & beanballs

Ingredients & Steps

13 balls

Cook and allow to cool completely

Bake at 350° until dry and cracked (~15 min)

After cooling, pulse into loose meal

Sauté

Pulse all ingredients together until dough forms

Form and refrigerate balls

Freeze leftovers; thaw overnight before cooking
