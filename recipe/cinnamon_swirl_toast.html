Cinnamon Swirl Toast

not too sweet = can’t stop eatin’

Ingredients & Steps

1 loaf

dough

Mix in stand mixer until smooth dough forms

Rise until doubled

filling

Grease loaf pan

Laminate dried fruit into dough

Flatten dough into 8"x20" rectangle


Egg wash the dough


spiced sugar

Leaving a 1" margin, sprinkle spiced sugar over dough

Roll the dough tightly and neatly

Seal and tuck the ends

Transfer seam side down to pan

Cover and proof until dough crests over the pan height

Egg wash

350° : 45 minutes

Sources

King Arthur

Notes

  • Have the starter ready by early morning
  • For simple cinnamon sugar, use 2 tsp of cinnamon
