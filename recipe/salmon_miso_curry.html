Salmon Miso Curry

cháo vịt vibes

Ingredients & Steps

4 servings

Cut into 2-inch chunks


Season and set aside

Sauté until softened and aromatic


Sauté until miso is slightly caramelized (2 minutes)


Boil for 5 minutes


Add vegetables and salmon, reduce heat to medium-low

Cook until salmon reaches 120°

Remove from heat


Stir in lime juice and season with any additional salt and pepper
