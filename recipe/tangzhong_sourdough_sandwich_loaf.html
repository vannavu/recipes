Tangzhong Sourdough Sandwich Loaf

tender despite being minimally enriched

Ingredients & Steps

1 loaf

levain

Rise until peak, 10–12 hours

tangzhong

One hour before levain peaks, whisk then heat until thickened

Cool in stand mixer bowl

dough

Mix on lowest speed to combine

Increase to speed 2 and mix until dough has developed enough strength to not immediately tear and uniformly clumps around the hook

Transfer to wetted bowl

bulk ferment

Let the dough rest and rise for another 3-7 hours, until jiggly with a smooth dome shape

shaping & final proof

Gently pre-shape dough into a round and rest uncovered for 30 minutes

Shape dough : fold two sides in → roll into tight log

Transfer to pullman loaf pan and proof (1.5-2 hours)

baking

Preheat oven to 425°

When oven is preheated, spray loaf with 5 spritzes of water and loosely cover with tin foil

Lower heat to 400° and bake for 30 minutes

Remove foil and bake for 20 minutes (internal 205°)

Remove loaf from pan and bake for another 5 minutes for extra color

Cool completely before slicing
