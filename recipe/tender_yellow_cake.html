Tender Yellow Cake

🧁 the best classic birthday cake 🧁

Ingredients & Steps

8" cake / 12 cupcakes / 36 mini cupcakes

dry ingredients

Whisk

Preheat oven to 350°

wet ingredients

Using a mixer, cream butter, oil, and sugar


Mix in eggs one by one


Mix until combined

Fold in dry ingredients

Distribute batter into pans

Tap pans to pop large air bubbles

Bake until cake springs back
• 8" round : 35–40 minutes
• cupcakes : 15–20 minutes
• mini cupcakes : 10 minutes

Sources

Notes

  • If doubling recipe, use 3 egg yolks
