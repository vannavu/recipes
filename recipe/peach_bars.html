Ingredients & Steps

8x8 pan

dry ingredients

Line pan with parchment

Preheat oven at 375°

Incorporate into dry ingredients to create a crumbly mixture

Press 3/4 of mixture into the pan

Tile peaches over shortbread base

Sprinkle remaining shortbread mixture on top

Finish with sliced almonds

375° : 30 minutes
