Rustic Sourdough Loaf

delightfully chewy loaf with a thin crust
( the best butter toast )

Ingredients & Steps

1 loaf

levain

Rise until ripe, 10-12 hours

dough

One hour before levain peaks, mix in stand mixer on lowest speed until combined

Autolyse for 1 hour

Incorporate into dough on lowest stand mixer speed

Increase to speed 2 and knead for 15 minutes, until the dough passes windowpane test

Transfer to wetted bowl

bulk ferment

Let the dough rest and rise 75% in volume

shaping & final proof

Gently pre-shape dough into a round and rest uncovered for 30 minutes

Grease cast iron loaf pan with butter

Shape dough : fold two sides in → roll into tight log

Transfer to loaf pan and proof (1-2 hours)

baking

Preheat oven to 450°

Using scissors, score dough 1/4" deep at an angle

When oven is preheated, spray loaf with 5 spritzes of water and loosely cover with tin foil

Bake for 30 minutes

Remove foil and lower heat to 400°, bake for 20 minutes

Remove loaf from pan and cool in oven overnight

Notes

  • Start of the levain to baked loaf : ~24 hours
  • Bulk ferment and proofing times depend on ambient temperature and starter strength
  • Spray bench scraper with water to prevent sticking
