Sourdough Naan

Ingredients & Steps

10 naan

Knead with silicone spatula until smooth, or do 3 sets of stretch and folds

Oil hands, shape dough into a ball, rest for 4+ hours

Divide dough into 10 balls, rest for at least 15 minutes

Heat carbon steel pan on medium-high heat

Lightly dust working surface with rice flour

Press dough ball into a disc, top with kalonji, and roll 1/8" thick

Spray plain side with water and slap into pan

Once naan begins to bubble, flip pan over burner to char
