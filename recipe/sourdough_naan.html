Ingredients & Steps

10 naan

Knead with silicone spatula

Oil hands, shape dough into a ball, rest until doubled and aerated (4+ hours)

Divide dough and shape, rest for 10 minutes

Lightly dust surface with rice flour

Roll 1/8" thick, press in topping

Spray plain side with water, slap onto carbon steel pan, and dimple dough

Flip pan over burner to char
