Vegan Taco Meat

ground lentils & mushrooms

Ingredients & Steps

6 servings

Boil for 5 minutes

Drain and rinse

Boil for an additional 3-5 minutes, until just tender

Drain well and roughly chop in food processor

Transfer to mixing bowl

Toast in pan

Chop in food processor into smaller pieces


Add to walnuts in food processor and chop

Transfer to mixing bowl with lentils

seasoning

Mix

Cook in two batches
