Ingredients & Steps

8 servings

Pan fry and set aside

Brown the shiitake


Create a well and brown the pork


Cook until onions are translucent


Cook until aromatic


Create a well in the pan for the chunjang and fry for 3 minutes


Add zucchini along with tofu and sweet potato

Mix to evenly distribute chunjang


Cover and simmer until vegetables are cooked


Simmer until thickened

Sources

The Foodie Takes Flight

Notes

  • If using frozen sweet potato, add later with the zucchini
