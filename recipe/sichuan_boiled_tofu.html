Sichuan Boiled Tofu

that plain ol’ sichuan good-good

Ingredients & Steps

5–6 servings

Slice, boil, drain

Fry peppercorn until fragrant

Remove peppercorns and coarsely grind for later use

Cook until shiitake begins to turn golden brown

Create a well in the shiitake to cook aromatics and paste

Cook for 1–2 minutes

Add tofu and stir fry to coat everything in the paste

Bring to a low boil

Heat until thickened

garnish

Sources

The Woks of Life

Notes

  • Serve with steamed or blanched greens like gai lan (cook greens first for a one pot meal)
