Buttermilk Butterfly Roast Chicken

bonus schmaltz-tatoes

Ingredients & Steps

6–8 servings

marinade

Marinate chicken for 24–48 hours

One hour before roasting the chicken, remove it from fridge

Toss potatoes and arrange skin side down on deep half sheet pan

Thoroughly wipe off marinade from chicken

Place chicken on baking rack over the potatoes

425° : 20 minutes

400° : 30+ minutes, until deepest part of the breast reaches 150°

Let chicken rest for 10 minutes before carving

Sources

Peter

Notes

  • Yogurt + water = 1.5 cups of buttermilk
  • Full 48-hour marinade works best
  • While the roasted chicken is resting, greens can be roasted among the schmaltz and potatoes
