Panettone

a holiday journey

Ingredients & Steps

6 jumbo cups

day 1 (4pm) : dough 1

Mix until combined

Rest for 24 hours

day 1 : aromatics

Mix and let rest

day 2 (4pm) : dough 2

Mix until combined


Mix until combined


Add yolks one at a time as they fully incorporate into the dough


Add butter a tablespoon at a time

Knead until sides of the bowl are clean

Proof at until triple the size (1+ hour)

day 2 (8:30pm) : dough 3

Mix until combined


Add yolks one at a time as they fully incorporate into the dough


Add butter a tablespoon at a time

Mix until sides of the bowl are clean


Mix until combined, sides of the bowl are clean, and dough is elastic

Prep baking cups

Transfer dough to a silicone pastry mat and stretch into a large rectangle


Sprinkle half of the chocolate onto the dough

Book fold

Sprinkle half of the remaining chocolate

Fold one third of the dough over the center

Sprinkle remaining chocolate

Fold last third over the center

Divide into 6 pieces and shape into balls, transferring to the baking cups

Cover and proof at room temp for 6+ hours, until dough nearly reaches the edge of the cup and has domed past the rim

Remove cover and let the dough rest until a skin has formed (10+ minutes)

day 3 (9am–12pm) : final bake

Preheat oven to 375°

As shallow as possible, score an X into the dough

Egg wash and sprinkle sugar over dough

Bake for 10 minutes, then lower heat to 350°

Bake until dark brown (20+ minutes / internal temp: 195°)

Turn off the oven and leave the door a quarter of the way open for the panettone to cool for 15 minutes

Remove panettone from the oven and cool completely (1.5+ hours)
