Mix until combined

Add butter a tablespoon at a time

Knead until sides of the bowl are clean

Proof at 80° until double to triple the size (4+ quarts)

day 2 (8:30pm) : dough 3

Mix until combined


Add yolks one at a time as they fully incorporate into the dough


Add butter a tablespoon at a time

Mix until sides of the bowl are clean


Mix until combined, sides of the bowl are clean, and dough is elastic

Prep baking cups

Transfer dough to a pastry board and stretch into a large rectangle


Sprinkle half of the chocolate onto the dough

Book fold

Sprinkle half of the remaining chocolate

Fold one third of the dough over the center

Sprinkle remaining chocolate

Fold last third over the center

Divide into 12 pieces and shape into balls, transferring to the baking cups

Cover and proof at room temp for 12+ hours, until dough nearly reaches the edge of the cup and has domed past the rim

day 3 (9am–12pm) : final bake

Preheat oven to 350°

Score dough

Egg wash and sprinkle sugar over dough

Bake until dark brown (internal temp: 195°)

Cool completely
