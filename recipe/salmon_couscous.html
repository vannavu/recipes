Salmon Couscous

refreshing 🐟🌿🍋

Ingredients & Steps

4 servings

Preheat oven to 275°

Line baking sheet with parchment paper

Oil and salt fish

Roast salmon skin side down until it reaches 120° (20–30 minutes)

Separate flesh from skin and crisp the skin at 350°

Using hands, flake the fish

Sauté in olive oil until soft


Sauté until lightly toasted and fragrant (1 minute)


Simmer until most of the broth has evaporated and the couscous is tender

Drain excess liquid

dressing

Mix in the same pot used to cook the couscous


greens

Add spinach then couscous, mix until spinach is wilted (return to heat if needed)


Fold in salmon, salmon skin, and herbs and season with salt and pepper

Serve warm or cold
