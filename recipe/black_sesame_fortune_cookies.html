Black Sesame Fortune Cookies

reminiscent of thin almond cookies

Ingredients & Steps

30 cookies

black sesame paste

Toast until fragrant

Transfer to measuring cup then pour into spice grinder


Process into a powder


Process into a paste, adding oil as necessary

Keep refrigerated

Whisk


Whisk

Refrigerate for 1 hour

Preheat oven to 350Â°

Prep two baking sheets with gridded silicone baking mats

Set up muffin tin, bowl or cup with a thin rim, and tablespoon scoop

Scoop a tablespoon of batter into the center of a 4x4" square, leaving an inch gap between each cookie (6 cookies to a half sheet pan)

Using a small offset spatula, spread each scoop of batter into a 4" round (it does not need to be perfectly even as the batter will spread while baking)

( Sprinkle with black sesame seeds )

Bake for 8+ minutes until the all the edges are browned

While a tray is baking, work on another tray

When cookies are done baking, flip the cookies

( Place fortunes )

Fold a round into a loose taco, then, using the rim of a cup, dent the bottom center and bend into fortune cookie shape

Transfer to muffin tin to retain the shape of the cookie as it cools

Store in an airtight container

Notes

  • For simpler cookies, leave as rounds
  • If the cookie tears during shaping or does not retain its snap, it was not baked long enough
  • If the cookies are too cool to be shaped, return to the oven to resoften
