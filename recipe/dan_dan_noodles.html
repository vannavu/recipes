Dan Dan Noodles

spicy so good

Ingredients & Steps

6 servings

chili oil

Heat oil and spices on medium-low until it reaches 325°

Remove from heat and rest for 7 minutes

Place pepper flakes in an 8 oz jar

Strain oil into pepper flakes and stir

pork

Brown ground pork

Add sauce and cook until liquid is evaporated

Sauté

sauce

Sources

The Woks of Life

Notes

  • 4/5 spice level. Adjust pepper flake blend for preferred spiciness. Gochugaru is essential for appetizing red color.
