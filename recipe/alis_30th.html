brownie

Prep 7" baking round with parchment and butter

Whisk


Whisk

Parbake until the surface begins to set

cheesecake

Remove oven rack and preheat oven to 425°

In stand mixer, mix until smooth


Mix until combined


Place a sieve over the mixing bowl and strain eggs into the batter

Mix on low until combined


Bring cream to a boil

Mix the batter on low while streaming in the heavy cream


Line oven rack with foil

Pour 1/2 of the batter into cake mold on top of the brownie

Sprinkle a layer of biscoff

Pour the remaining batter

Remove any bubbles by bouncing the back of a spoon on the surface

Bake until puffed, beginning to brown, and edges are dry

Turn off the oven and crack the door for 10 minutes

Heat the oven at 250°

Bake until the center is 150°

Cool for 15 minutes then run an offset knife around the edges to loosen the cake

Cool at room temp for 1–4 hours

Cover and refrigerate for 12+ hours
