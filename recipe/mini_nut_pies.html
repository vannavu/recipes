Mini Nut Pies

scrumptious

Ingredients & Steps

24 mini pies

crust

Roll the crust 1/8" thick and punch out 2.75" rounds

Press into the mini cupcake pan and chill until stiff

Parbake at 325° : 7 minutes

Remove from oven and immediately fill

Raise oven temp to 350°

filling : pecan

Whisk


Fold into the syrup


( Line bottoms of pie crust with chocolate )

Fill crusts with 1/2 tbsp of filling


350° : 16+ minutes

filling : salted honey nut

Toast and cool


Mix over medium-low heat until sugar dissolves

Increase heat to medium and swirl the pan as it rapidly simmers and slightly thickens (~5 minutes)

Remove from heat


Stir into syrup


Stir sesame seeds and nuts into syrup

Fill crusts with 1/2 tbsp of filling


350° : 16+ minutes
