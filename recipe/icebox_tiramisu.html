Icebox Tiramisu

light yet indulgent

Ingredients & Steps

12 servings

Lightly wet a loaf pan, then line with two pieces of criss-crossed plastic wrap that are long enough for overhang

cream filling

Over medium heat, stir coffee mixture and bring to a boil


On medium speed, gradually whisk coffee syrup into egg yolks

Increase speed to medium-high and whip until mixture is light, thick, mousse-like and cooled to room temp

Transfer to another bowl


Whip to stiff peaks

On low speed, add coffee mousse to the whipped cream mixture, then increase speed to medium-high until stiff peaks form

For each ladyfingers layer, trim cookies as needed and move quickly to lightly soak each cookie

Assemble layers:
• 70g cream filling
• ~7 ladyfingers
• 140g cream filling
• ~7 ladyfingers
• 140g cream filling
• ~7 ladyfingers
• remaining cream filling

Fold over plastic wrap to cover

Chill for 12-24 hours

Unmold cake onto dish

Whip cream to medium peaks

Dollop all of the whipped cream onto the cake and gently spread to cover the cake


Right before serving, lightly dust with cocoa powder

The frosted tiramisu should be served immediately or within 4 hours of chilling in the fridge

Sources

Claire Saffitz

Notes

  • Country Crock plant cream creates a significantly more stable whipped cream that allows the fully-assembled tiramisu to hold up in the fridge for a couple days
