Bánh Bò Nướng

chewy cake with a balanced sweetness & coconut-pandan flavor

Ingredients & Steps

1 loaf (12 slices)

pandan paste

Juice and allow sediment to settle overnight in the fridge

Remove water

wet ingredients

Preheat loaf pan at 375°

Gently heat mixture on low to dissolve sugar and melt coconut oil

Mix flavorings into coconut mixture, then set aside to cool to lukewarm

dry ingredients

In a bowl separate from the wet ingredients, whisk dry ingredients together

Using a spatula, work eggs through a fine mesh sieve into the coconut mixture

Stir to combine

Pour half of the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and use a spatula to mix until flour on the sides of the bowl are roughly incorporated (batter will be lumpy)

Stir in the remaining half of the wet ingredients

Strain batter through a sieve and work out the the lumps

Stir batter

Pour into preheated pan

Tap pan ten times to remove major air bubbles

Bake for 35 minutes (until thoroughly browned and internal temperature reaches 210°)

Cool upside down until cool enough to handle (~30 minutes)

Remove from pan and cool completely on wire rack

Slice and refrigerate leftovers

Sources

Scruff & Steph

Notes

  • Pandan paste can be substituted with 1/4+ tsp gel pandan extract
