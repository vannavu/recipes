Bánh Bò Nướng

chewy cake with a balanced sweetness & coconut-pandan flavor

Ingredients & Steps

1 loaf (12 slices)

wet ingredients

Gently heat mixture to dissolve sugar and melt coconut oil

Mix flavorings into coconut mixture, then set aside to cool to lukewarm

dry ingredients

In a bowl separate from the wet ingredients, sift flour and create a well

Grease bottom and corners of loaf pan

Preheat pan at 375° (350° for convection ovens)

Using a spatula, work eggs through a fine mesh sieve into the coconut mixture

Stir to combine

While whisking, stream wet ingredients into the dry ingredients

After breaking up large lumps, strain mixture through a sieve and work out the rest of the lumps

While stirring mixture, sift rising agents into the batter

Strain mixture again

Pour into preheated pan

Tap pan to pop bubbles

Bake for 35 minutes (until thoroughly browned and internal temperature reaches 210°)

Cool upside down until cool enough to handle (~30 minutes)

Remove from pan and continue cooling on wire rack

Slice and refrigerate leftovers

Sources

Scruff & Steph

Notes

  • Coconut oil can be substituted with 8g of vegetable oil
