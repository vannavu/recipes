Black Sesame Rice Krispies Treats

sweet + toasty + nutty

Ingredients & Steps

16 bars

Grease 8x8 pan with butter

Brown the butter


Toast the sesame seeds in the browned butter until fragrant


Melt marshmallows over low heat


Mix then pat into pan

Cool completely then cut (1 hour)
