Gochujang Cheese Rolls

ayyy

Ingredients & Steps

12 small rolls

tangzhong

Whisk and heat until thickened

dough

Mix on lowest setting to combine ingredients

Increase to speed 2 and mix until dough is smooth and windowpanes

Proof for 1 hour then refrigerate 12-24 hours

filling

Grease 9x13" pan with butter

Roll dough into 19x10" rectangle

Spread thin layer of gochujang, leaving an inch margin on one long end

Flatten margin


Gently press fillings into the dough

Roll

Trim 1/2" off the ends

Cut twelve 1.5" slices

Arrange into 4x3 grid in pan

Proof

topping

Preheat oven to 375°

Egg wash

Bake for 20–25 minutes until golden brown (internal temp 195°)


Mix and brush over rolls


Brush over rolls


Sprinkle on top
