Steamed Egg

silken tofu texture

Ingredients & Steps

2 servings

Steam the eggs in a covered bowl on medium-high heat for 20 minutes, or until the egg is set

Sources

Woks of Life

Notes

  • Amount of liquid is 1–1.5x the volume of eggs (one egg = 50mL)
  • Water can be replaced with broth
  • Decrease amount of liquid for firmer egg
  • Glass container + silicone lid works well
