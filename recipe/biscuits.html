Biscuits

classic rough layers

( ideal for biscuits & gravy )

Ingredients & Steps

9 biscuits

Line baking pan

Mix dry ingredients


Cut butter into flour

Whisk

Add to flour mixture and fold into a crumbly dough

Shape dough into a rectangle

Cut into thirds, layer sections on top of each other, flatten and repeat

Shape into 10"x6" rectangle (about 1 inch thick)

Trim edges then cut 8 rectangles

Gather scraps into another biscuit

Chill biscuits in fridge while oven preheats to 425°

Bake for 14–15 minutes, until golden brown

Sources

Kenji López-Alt

Notes

  • 2 oz sour cream + 2 oz milk = 120mL buttermilk
