Soft Sourdough Loaf

tender despite being minimally enriched

Ingredients & Steps

1 loaf

levain

Rise until peak, 10–12 hours

yudane

dough

Mix and check temp to determine water temp


Mix on lowest speed to combine

Increase to speed 2 and mix until dough has developed enough strength to not immediately tear and uniformly clumps around the hook

Transfer to wetted bowl with volume markings

bulk ferment

Let the dough rest and rise 50% in volume (1.5-4 hours)

shaping & final proof

Pre-shape dough into a round and rest uncovered for 30 minutes

Shape dough : fold two sides in → roll into tight log

Transfer to pullman loaf pan and proof until dough crests to the height of the pan (1.5-2 hours)

baking

Preheat oven to 425°

When oven is preheated, spray loaf with 5 spritzes of water and loosely cover with tin foil

Lower heat to 400° and bake for 30 minutes

Remove foil and bake for 20 minutes (internal 205°)

Cool completely before slicing (2 hours)
