Flour Tortillas

worth it

Ingredients & Steps

20+ 6" tortillas / 12+ 8" tortillas

Mix and knead until a ball comes together (if too sticky, gradually add more flour)

Portion and shape dough into balls
• 6" tortilla : 1 oz
• 8" tortilla : 1.5 oz

Cover and rest the dough until it is relaxed enough to roll (30+ minutes)

Preheat cast iron or carbon steel pan on medium heat

Lightly flour work surface

Roll dough very thin (~1/16")

Cook each side until bubbly and lightly browned

Sources

King Arthur

Notes

  • Keep tortillas soft and warm by lining a plastic bag or lidded container with a single layer of flour sack towel
  • Leftovers store best in the fridge for a couple days; reheat with a spritz of water
