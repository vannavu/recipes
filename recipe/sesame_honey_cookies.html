Sesame Honey Cookies

thin, chewy, fragrant, nutty

Ingredients & Steps

18 cookies

wet ingredients

Cool mixture until it stays homogenous and slightly thickens
Whisk by hand

dry ingredients

Chill for 3-4 hours

Portion dough and cover in sesame

Chill until firm (3-4 hours)

Bake 6 cookies to a pan

350° : 12–14 min (until golden brown all over)

Cool completely

Sources

easygayoven

Notes

  • If frozen, thaw in refrigerator before baking
  • For a simpler process, sesame seeds can be mixed into the dough with the other dry ingredients, yielding ~20 cookies. The cookies will be slightly thicker than when rolled in sesame.
