Soymilk

rich & creamy

Ingredients & Steps

32 fl oz

Soak soybeans in a covered three quart pot for 1.5 hours

Drain water and pour only enough of the filtered water to cover the soybeans

Use an immersion blender to break up most of the soybeans

Transfer to 4 cup measuring glass

Place a fine mesh strainer over the pot, which the milk will strain into

Run blended soybeans through a juicer

Mix some of the filtered water with the pulp and run it through the juicer again, gradually pouring the remaining water into the juicer to help process the pulp

On medium-high heat, frequently stir the soymilk and bring it to a gentle boil

Reduce heat to medium and simmer for 10 minutes while continuing to stir

Strain soymilk and top off with any additional water to reach 32 fl oz
