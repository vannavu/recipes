Sourdough Loaf

the best butter toast
( rustic & chewy loaf with a thin crust )

Ingredients & Steps

1 loaf

levain

Rise until ripe, 10-12 hours

* ambient temperature : water temperature
• 68° : 98°
• 70° : 94°
• 72° : 90°
• 74° : 86°
• 76° : 82°
• 78° : 78°

Mix in stand mixer on lowest speed until combined

Autolyse for 1 hour

(Use remaining levain as starter)

Incorporate into autolyse on lowest stand mixer speed

Knead dough on speed 2 for 15-20 minutes

Transfer to wetted bowl


Bulk ferment (4-8 hours)

Pre-shape dough into a round and rest until relaxed

Grease loaf pan with butter

Shape dough : envelope fold + cinch + log

Transfer to loaf pan and proof (1-2 hours)

Preheat oven to 450°

Using scissors, score dough 1/4" deep

When oven is preheated, spray loaf with 4 spritzes of water and loosely cover with tin foil

Bake for 25 minutes

Remove foil and lower heat to 400°, bake for 20 minutes

Remove loaf from pan and cool in oven overnight

Sources

Notes

  • Start of the levain to baked loaf : ~24 hours
  • Bulk ferment and proofing times depend on temperature
  • Spray bench scraper with water to prevent sticking
  • Use rice flour on work surface and for the surface of the dough
