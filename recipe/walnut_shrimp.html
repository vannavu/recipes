Walnut Shrimp

skeptical

Ingredients & Steps

4 servings

candied walnuts

Boil water and blanch walnuts for 1 minute

Wash with cold water to remove skins


Toss walnuts in sugar


Combine and set aside

Preheat 2 inches of oil to 350°

Line a sheet pan with parchment paper

Prepare a wire rack to drain the walnuts

In batches, fry the walnuts until they start to turn dark brown and candy (~1 minute)

Transfer from the wire rack to a single layer on the parchment paper

Evenly sprinkle the sesame mixture over the walnuts

Cool completely (~30 minutes)

sauce

Whisk in a bowl large enough to toss the shrimp

broccoli & oranges

Cut into shrimp sized florets

Blanch for 2 minutes

Season with salt and MSG


Cut into supremes

fried shrimp

Preheat 3 inches of oil to 350°

Prep wire rack for shrimp to drain on


Put cornstarch into a shallow dish

Once oil is heated, dust a few shrimp with cornstarch

Fry in batches until opaque and curled up (~2 minutes)

Once all the shrimp are cooked, toss in the sauce

Toss in the broccoli and oranges

Top with toasted sesame and walnuts
