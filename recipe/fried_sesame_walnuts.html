Fried Sesame Walnuts

thin glaze, tender nut

Ingredients & Steps

2 cups

seasoning

Combine and set aside

Soak in boiled water for 3 minutes

Wash with cold water

Peel skins


Toss walnuts in sugar


Preheat oil to 350Â°

On a half sheet pan, prepare a wire rack to drain the walnuts

In batches, fry the walnuts until they start to turn dark brown

Transfer to the wire rack, separating the walnuts from each other

Once all the walnuts are fried, transfer them to a 9x13 pan

Evenly sprinkle the seasoning over the walnuts then toss to coat

Cool completely (~30 minutes) before storing

Discard the oil

Notes

  • Best eaten within a week
